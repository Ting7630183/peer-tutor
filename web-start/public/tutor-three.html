Warm Tips

As a tutor, you have to be careful with how you speak. You might have mastered this part of the knowledge, but it’s still difficult for other peers. As mentioned previously, teaching others helps you consolidate your understanding as well. So take this experience as an opportunity to learn!

Some encouraging phrases can be helpful.Instead of saying “you’re wrong” or “you’re stupid”, you can try:


Student got a wrong answer.
Student missed a step within the problem.
Encouraging a break for the student.
